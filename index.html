The Problem

AI is making decisions.
Who is accountable?

Enterprises deploy agents into production — approving transactions, modifying systems, interacting with customers. No system answers who approved it, why it was made, or whether it can be reconstructed.

Explore the Vision →
Who approved this decision?
No structured approval. No named approver. When regulators ask, the answer is silence.
Why was it made?
Decisions emerge from a black box. The reasoning is not persisted — and cannot be reproduced.
Can we reconstruct it?
What ran, what policies applied, what the agent saw — none of it is queryable after the fact.
